Final Revision

12/12/2018

Welcome to the end. We made it; the semester is over! Today we present the final "polished" version of the website.


Updates Include:


  • Directors Page with all directors of most viewed
  • Locations page removed.
  • Links fixed for pages that didn't redirect
  • Favicon added for ALL pages this time... I promise..

I had to change the scope again of the website. I realized that creating a location page with every single area that was being filmed, plus link to every movie would be impossible. This is due to time constraints, and just having not much time to do this with finals coming up and finishing. I really wanted to have a search where you could search your ZIP or location and have it show up. However, I had no way of allowing that on a github hosted website. I'm fairly certain that I couldn't run a PERL script on this, which would make it fairly impossible with my knowledge of web programming.


Without further ado... I welcome you to Shot4Shot.



Best,

Michael

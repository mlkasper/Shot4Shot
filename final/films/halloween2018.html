Halloween (2018)


Synopsis:
It's been 40 years since Laurie Strode survived a vicious attack from crazed killer Michael Myers on Halloween night. Locked up in an institution, Myers manages to escape when his bus transfer goes horribly wrong. Laurie now faces a terrifying showdown when the masked madman returns to Haddonfield, Ill. -- but this time, she's ready for him




The new Halloween is also set in the fictional town of Haddonfield (Illinois) as the original one, and it was shot on location in Charleston, South Carolina.

The Film opens on Dana Haines (Rhian Rees) and Aaron Korey (Jefferson Hall) entering the mental facility where Michael Meyers is kept. He's been moved around many times but this has been his home for many years.

The courtyard at North Charleston's Military Magnet Academy Magnolia Cemetery in Charleston. Danny Jones Complex Armory in North Charleston Gas Station, 1256 Yeamans Hall Rd, Hanahan SC Laurie's home is on private land, located in a rural area of North Charleston off Palmetto Commerce Parkway

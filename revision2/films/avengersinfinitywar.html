Avengers: Infinity War


Synopsis:
Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk and the rest of the Avengers unite to battle their most powerful enemy yet -- the evil Thanos. On a mission to collect all six Infinity Stones, Thanos plans to use the artifacts to inflict his twisted will on reality. The fate of the planet and existence itself has never been more uncertain as everything the Avengers have fought for has led up to this moment.




Avenges: Infinity War was largely shot on a sound stage, and having mostly CGI effects. However, some scenes were shot on location. The entire battle sequence with Wanda and Vision in Edinburgh was shot on location, however the rest of the movie is purely CGI and sound stages.

Old Scottish Parliament, Edinburgh Scottland

This sequence is where Midnight Proxima and Wanda Maximoff are facing off against eachother for Vision. Eventually the entire Ex-Avengers (including Captain America, Falcon, and Blackwidow) show up to help Wanda and vision battle The Children of Thanos.
